BBVA Mwanzo Program

Do you have an idea you want to turn into a viable business?

BBVA's Mwanzo program is the first step to help get you there. It is a 4-week, hybrid and virtual program for Black Founders looking to bring their business ideas to life.

Program is carried out all year round with ongoing enrollment. It's a fun way to start your entrepreneurial journey. The Mwanzo program is at no cost to you.
