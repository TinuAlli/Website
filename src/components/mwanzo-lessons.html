What will you learn?

Mwanzo program is designed to provide you with the essential tools and skills to get clarity on your idea and take the following steps to get towards your founder's journey. During the program, we will focus on these critical areas:

1. Ideation

Learn how to package a business idea that sells and is precisely what customers want and investors like.

2. Planning

Simplify your vision and establish your goals in a one-page plan for funders and partners.

3. Finance

Learn a simple way to create a financial model in 20 minutes without excel magic or an accountant.

4. Selling

Learn how to sell your idea to others and let them join you to invest time, money, and effort.
