Requirements

Who can apply?

The ideal candidates for the BBVA's Mwanzo program must have:

High Motivation

Be motivated to bring your idea to life and be ready to commit time to develop your business.

Compelling Problem

Have a business idea that is solving a compelling problem using technology.

Coachable

Be coachable, ambitious, and collaborative.

Black-led

Have at least one Black-led founder or co-founder.
