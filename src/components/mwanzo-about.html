What does Mwanzo Mean?

Mwanzo means "new beginnings" or "genesis" in Swahili. In the Mwanzo program, we support you with the tools to help you take action on your idea so you can launch your business.

Hybrid & Virtual

4-week, live and on-demand workshops combined with live 1:1 coaching sessions throughout the program.

For Aspiring Entrepreneurs

Mwanzo Program brings together aspiring Black entrepreneurs, creatives, visionaries, and innovators from across Canada to start profitable businesses.
