Black Seed Accelerator Program

This program is designed to address the needs of Black-led tech and tech enabled startups. From gaining support through the BBVA Playbooks to accessing funding to scale your business.

What you Learn:

  • How to design business models.
  • How to test whether an idea is worth pursuing.
  • How to communicate an idea clearly and concisely to internal and external stakeholders.
  • How to identify and prioritize the riskiest assumptions in your business model.
  • How to stress test your riskiest assumptions using small and fast experiments.
  • How to use customer interviews to learn from customers.
  • How to build a company-wide dashboard.
  • How to pitch to customers.
  • How to operate and make decisions under conditions of extreme uncertainty.

Bonus:

  1. Bi-weekly coaching calls
  2. Homework & Assignments
  3. 1-1 Sessions
  4. Pop-up workshops including raising capital as a Black founder
  5. Office hours with BBVA Director
