Our Vision

We believe Black people should be in tech

BBVA recognises that there is a significant diversity gap in the Canadian technology ecosystem.
BBVA envisions a vibrant Canadian technology ecosystem with significant participation by the members of the Black community.

Our Mission

Add more Black-led companies and more Black angels

Black Led

Our goal is to add 1,000 Black-led companies by 2030.

Black Angels

Our goal is to increase the number of angel investors from the Black community.
